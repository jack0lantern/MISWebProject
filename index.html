Who am I?

My name is John Clinton Tuttle but I go by Clint just to confuse people at the DMV. I recently became a full-time Lecturer for the McCombs School of Business in the IROM Department. Prior to Aug 2012 I only lectured part-time and was an Independent Marketing Consultant part-time as well. Prior to 2011 I spent 9 years at Accenture as an IT Consultant. Outside of work I am a husband to an amazing woman and a dog owner of the coolest dogs ever. One of my passions is music so during some weekends and summer break, I spend time playing music at youth camps and retreats...a far cry from my days at Blind Pig Pub on 6th Street. I recently moved back to Austin in August 2012 but before that I was living in Laredo for a year and Boston for three years. I enjoy UT Football, watching movies, exercising, playing with my dogs, watching live music, trying new Austin local brews and cooking with my wife.
